Seraphim12 Foundation

Seraphim12 Foundation is a non-profit dedicated to ending the abuse, neglect and slaughter of American horses. The primary objective of this website is to inspire compassion through the use of beautiful photography, while informing visitors about the issues and how to take action.

Visit the website

This WordPress website was built using the Themify framework, customized to suit the organization's needs, including:

  • Responsive design for all devices
  • Parallax effects
  • Blog
  • Donations page
  • PopVox letter-writing integration
  • E-commerce shop (not yet live)
  • Fly-in animations and modals
  • Constant Contact integration
  • Social media integration

I worked with Seraphim12 Foundation as a kind of "Chief Cook and Bottlewasher." Other services provided are:

  • Events co-ordinator - fundraising events, including an annual Awareness Day with The Mets at Citi Field.
  • Graphic design for flyers, inserts, postcards, invitations, one-sheets
  • Copy for press releases & blog
  • Ebasts - design & copy
  • Social media strategy
  • Google AdWords campaign
  • Google analytics and SEO
  • Photography
  • Small video edits
  • Budgetary consultation
  • Volunteer coordinator
