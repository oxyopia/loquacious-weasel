New York Equine

New York Equine, a veterinary clinic specializing in the care and treatment of horses, wanted a website to connect with and educate their clientelle.

This custom WordPress site also integrated LifeLearn articles on various horse illnesses and ailments to educate their horse owning clientelle.

Visit the website

This WordPress website was built using the Pagelines framework, customized to suit the company's needs, including:

  • Rollover effects
  • Integration of LifeLearn articles covering various horse illnesses and ailments
  • Blog prepared for future use
