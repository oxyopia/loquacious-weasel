RGI, Inc.

RGI, Inc. is a homegoods company devoted to great design, sustainable materials, and giving back to the global communities essential to creating baskets and other storage solutions. They were looking for a clean and modern design.

The website was live for a very short time, as the company decided a complete rebranding was in order.

  • Multiple slideshows
  • Blog prepared for future use
